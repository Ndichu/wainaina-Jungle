Hon Wainaina wa Jungle Endorsement 2022

Friday, June 10th 2022 | 6:30 - 9:30 PM 

KiKICC Grounds in Kiambu Kenya

       
       I believe we can provide jobs to the jobless, homes to the homeless, and reclaim young people from violence and despair. I believe that we have a righteous wind at our backs and that as we stand on the crossroads of history, we can make the right choices, and meet the challenges that face us.

Our raisin detre is to transform Kiambu into a modern town with an attractive quality of life and a strong economy that is business and development focus. We are firmly aware that this transformation requires strong man of courage to provide firm and strong leadership.

       Corruption has been tolerated in Kenya for too long. It distorts our economy, frustrates our progress and damages our reputation. It is time to get tough on those who seek to use their positions of power for their own personal gain. We will clean up Government by introducing some of the toughest anti- corruption legislation in the world.

       Come and take part in the endorsement as a Kiambu governor on Friday, June 10th  2022. All are welcome dont hesitate to come with a friend.  

Kind regards,
Hon. Wainaina wa Jungle
